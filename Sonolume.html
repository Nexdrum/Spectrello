About Sonolume

Sonolume is an experimental platform for designing new musical interfaces that connect sound and light through visual structure.

Projects like Nebular and Spectrello explore harmonic relationships, instrument geometry, and new ways to interact with music. Future instruments and visual tools will continue expanding the system.
